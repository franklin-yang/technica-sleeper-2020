Technica is calling on students from all around the country to work alongside our 2020 organizing team!

As a Technica Campus Ambassador, you’ll have the unique opportunity to help foster our community beyond Technica weekend at UMD. As a representative of your school, you’ll spread the word about Technica 2020 on your campus and promote our message of inclusion in STEM for the largest all-women and non-binary hackathon in the world!

What do Campus Ambassadors do?

  • Generate awareness, excitement, and buzz leading up to Technica 2020
  • Coordinate bus pick-up locations from your school to Technica
  • Promote Technica on your campus and social media
  • Communicate with Technica’s Brand team weekly
